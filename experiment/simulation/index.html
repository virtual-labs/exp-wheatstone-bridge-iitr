Wheatstone Bridge


STEP 1 : Make all the Correct Connections.

1.    M1 of MCB - Main1

1.    M2 of MCB - Main2

STEP 2 : Click on "CHECK" Button.

STEP 3 : Click on the MCB to Turn ON the circuit.

STEP 4 : Set 'initial-final' switch on initial by clicking on rectangular red button.

STEP 5 : Set each knob from dropdown such that galvanometer shows 0 deflection.

STEP 6 : Now push the RED circular button to check whether the condition is balanced or not.

STEP 7 : Set initial-final switch on final by clicking on rectangular button again.

STEP 8 : Check the same readings on final condition by clicking the circular button again.

STEP 9 : Now if condition is balanced on both initial and final state Note down the readings of the MULTIPLIERS and OHMS.

STEP 10 : Click "ADD" button to add the values to the Observation Table.

STEP 11 : If condition is not balanced choose another combination of knobs.

STEP 12 : Add different values to the Table by selecting different value of multipliers.

STEP 13 : Click on "PRINT" button to print the page.
