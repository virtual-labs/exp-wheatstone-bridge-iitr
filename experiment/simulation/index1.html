Kelvin Double Bridge



KELVIN DOUBLE BRIDGE KIT

GALVANOMETER

Follow these instructions

STEP 1 : Make all the Correct Connections.

1.    M1 of MCB - M3 of KelvinKit     2.  M2 of MCB - M4 of KelvinKit

3.    C+ of KelvinKit - P+ of KelvinKit     4.  P- of KelvinKit - C- of KelvinKit

5.    C- of KelvinKit - C+ of KelvinKit

STEP 2 : Click on "CHECK" Button.     STEP 3 : Click on the MCB to Turn ON the circuit.

STEP 4 : Select the number from MULTIPLIERS and OHMS from both dropdown.

STEP 5 : Now push the RED buttom to check whether the combination is correct or not.

STEP 6 : If combination is correct perform STEPS 7 and 8 otherwise perform STEP 9

STEP 7 : Note down the readings of the MULTIPLIERS and OHMS when Galvanometer shows zero deflection.

STEP 8 : Click "ADD" button to add the values to the Observation Table.

STEP 9 : Select the different combinations from MULTIPLIERS and OHMS from both dropdown.

STEP 10 : Add different values to the Table by selecting different value of multipliers.

STEP 11 : Click on "PRINT" button to print the page.
